GTA ViSA

Release Date: June 2023

Genre: Open World

Platform: PC

Description:

Grand Theft Auto : V in San Andreas
Its a modpack that changes Grand Theft Auto : San Andreas to make it look like Grand Theft Auto : V
This modpack has a lot of modifications which provide a better experience within the game like V Character / Character Switch / V Hud and lot of things

Features:

  • V Hud (Radar - Menu - Status -...)
  • V Style (Character Switch)
  • V Graphic (Better Graphic than before but if you have a low-end device you can edit it)
  • V Texture (New Texture on Los Santos map only)
  • V Animation and Physics (New Animation and Physics like GTA V)
  • V Sounds (SFX and sounds of game has changed like GTA V vehicles and weapons)
  • V Modifications (Peds and Vehicles and Weapons have been changed to look like V)

System Requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows XP or newer
  • Processor: 2Ghz or more
  • Memory: 2GB RAM
  • Graphics: 512Mb or higher
  • Storage: 7Gb (gta + mod)
